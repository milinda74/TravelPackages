Our Story

Sri Lanka Travel Promise was founded with a simple mission: to share the beauty and wonder of Sri Lanka with travelers from around the world. Our team of passionate travel experts has curated the best experiences that this beautiful island has to offer.

From the pristine beaches of the south coast to the misty mountains of the hill country, from ancient cultural sites to thrilling wildlife adventures, we promise to deliver unforgettable experiences that will create memories to last a lifetime.
